Easy Spanish Sentences to Sound Like a Native Speaker

Learning simple Spanish sentences is a great way to start speaking confidently. These easy Spanish sentences will help you get your point across without fuss. Practise them with Lexa AI and download the app on the App Store here.

Common Spanish Phrases

Phrase (Textbook)How Locals Actually Say ItHow to Pronounce ItMeaning / English Translation
Me gusta la música.Me encanta la música.meh ehnkahntah lah mooseekah.I like music / I love music.
Tengo hambre.Muero de hambre.mooehroh deh ahmbreh.I'm hungry / I'm starving.
Estoy cansado.Estoy agotado.ehstohy ahgohtahdoh.I'm tired / I'm exhausted.
Hace frío.Está helando.ehstah ehlahndoh.It's cold / It's freezing.
Tengo sed.Estoy sediento.ehstohy sehdeeehntoh.I'm thirsty.
Estoy perdido.Estoy perdida/o.ehstohy pehrdeedah/oh.I'm lost.
Necesito ayuda.Necesito una mano.nehkehseetoh oonah mahnoh.I need help / I need a hand.
Voy al trabajo.Voy a la chamba.bohy ah lah kahmbah.I'm going to work.
Quiero comer.Tengo ganas de comer.tehngoh gahnahs deh kohmehr.I want to eat.
Estoy feliz.Estoy contento/a.ehstohy kohntehntoh/ah.I'm happy.
