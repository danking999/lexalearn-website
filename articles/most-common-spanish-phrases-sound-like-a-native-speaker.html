Most Common Spanish Phrases to Sound Like a Native Speaker

These are the most common Spanish phrases you’ll hear every day. Master them to sound natural, and practice with Lexa AI – download the app on the App Store here.

Essential Spanish Phrases

Phrase (textbook)How locals actually say itHow to pronounce itMeaning / English translation
Hola.¡Hola!¡ohlah!Hello.
¿Cómo estás?¿Qué tal?¿keh tahl?How are you?
Bien, gracias.Todo bien, gracias.tohdoh beeehn, grahkeeahs.Fine, thank you.
Gracias.Muchas gracias.mookahs grahkeeahs.Thank you / Thanks a lot.
Por favor.Porfa.pohrfah.Please.
Lo siento.Perdón.pehrdohn.I'm sorry.
No entiendo.No lo entiendo.noh loh ehnteeehndoh.I don't understand it.
¿Hablas inglés?¿Sabes inglés?¿sahbehs eenglehs?Do you speak English?
Adiós.Chao.kahoh.Goodbye.
Hasta luego.Nos vemos luego.nohs behmohs looehgoh.See you later.
