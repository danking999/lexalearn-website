Simple Spanish Sayings: Sound Like a Native Speaker

These simple Spanish sayings are easy to remember and carry timeless wisdom. Add them to your vocabulary and practice them with Lexa AI. Download the app on the App Store here.

Common Spanish Sayings

Phrase (textbook)How locals actually say itHow to pronounce itMeaning / English translation
Más vale tarde que nunca.Mejor tarde que nunca.mehhohr tahrdeh keh noonkah.Better late than never.
A caballo regalado no le mires el diente.A caballo regalado no se le mira el colmillo.ah kahbahyoh rehgahlahdoh noh seh leh meerah ehl kohlmeeyoh.Don’t look a gift horse in the mouth.
Al mal tiempo, buena cara.A mal tiempo, buena cara.ah mahl teeehmpoh, booehnah kahrah.Put on a brave face during bad times.
No hay mal que por bien no venga.Todo pasa por algo.tohdoh pahsah pohr ahlgoh.Every cloud has a silver lining.
El que busca, encuentra.Quien busca, encuentra.keeehn booskah, ehnkooehntrah.He/she who looks will find.
Ojos que no ven, corazón que no siente.Ojos que no ven, corazón que no siente.ohhohs keh noh behn, kohrahsohn keh noh seeehnteh.Out of sight, out of mind.
De tal palo, tal astilla.De tal padre, tal hijo.deh tahl pahdreh, tahl eehoh.Like father, like son.
A quien madruga, Dios le ayuda.Al que madruga, Dios le ayuda.ahl keh mahdroogah, deeohs leh ahyoodah.The early bird gets the worm.
Dime con quién andas y te diré quién eres.Con quien andas, eso eres.kohn keeehn ahndahs, ehsoh ehrehs.You are the company you keep.
El que no llora, no mama.El que no pide, no recibe.ehl keh noh peedeh, noh rehkeebeh.If you don’t ask, you don’t get.
