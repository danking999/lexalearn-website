Romantic Spanish Phrases: Sound Like a Native Speaker

Want to charm someone special? These romantic Spanish phrases will help you express love with ease. Practise them with Lexa AI, and download the app on the App Store here.

Common Romantic Phrases

Phrase (textbook)How locals actually say itHow to pronounce itMeaning / English translation
Te quiero.Te amo.teh ahmoh.I love you.
Me encantas.Me fascinas.meh fahskeenahs.I really like you.
Eres hermosa/o.Estás preciosa/o.ehstahs prehkeeohsah/oh.You look gorgeous.
Tu sonrisa ilumina mi día.Tu sonrisa alegra mi día.too sohnreesah ahlehgrah mee deeah.Your smile makes my day happy.
Tengo ganas de abrazarte.Quiero darte un abrazo.keeehroh dahrteh oon ahbrahsoh.I want to give you a hug.
Me haces falta.Te necesito.teh nehkehseetoh.I need you.
Eres todo para mí.Lo eres todo para mí.loh ehrehs tohdoh pahrah mee.You are my everything.
Tus ojos me enamoran.Tus ojos me hechizan.toos ohhohs meh ehkeesahn.Your eyes enchant me.
Quiero estar a tu lado para siempre.Quiero estar contigo siempre.keeehroh ehstahr kohnteegoh seeehmpreh.I want to be with you forever.
Eres mi razón de ser.Eres mi motivo.ehrehs mee mohteeboh.You are my reason.
