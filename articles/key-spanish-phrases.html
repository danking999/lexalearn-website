Key Spanish Phrases You Need to Know

Learning a new language can be exciting and rewarding. To get started, here are some key Spanish phrases that will help you communicate effectively in various situations.

Basic Greetings

  • Hola - Hello
  • Buenos días - Good morning
  • Buenas tardes - Good afternoon
  • Adiós - Goodbye

Common Courtesies

  • Por favor - Please
  • Gracias - Thank you
  • De nada - You’re welcome

Essential Questions

  • ¿Cómo estás? - How are you?
  • ¿Dónde está …? - Where is …?
  • ¿Cuánto cuesta? - How much does it cost?

These key Spanish phrases are just the beginning! Incorporating them into your daily conversations will greatly enhance your learning experience.
