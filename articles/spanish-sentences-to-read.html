Effective Spanish Sentences to Read for Language Learning

Reading and repeating Spanish sentences to read builds comprehension and confidence. The sentences below are easy to read and use in everyday life. Practise them with Lexa AI—download the app on the App Store here.

Readable Sentences and Colloquial Versions

Below are some common Spanish sentences along with their colloquial counterparts:

  • Textbook Sentence: Estoy aprendiendo español
    Colloquial Version: Estoy estudiando español
    Meaning: I’m learning Spanish
  • Textbook Sentence: Me gusta
    Colloquial Version: Me encanta
    Meaning: I like it / I love it
  • Textbook Sentence: ¿Dónde está el baño?
    Colloquial Version: ¿Dónde queda el baño?
    Meaning: Where is the bathroom?
  • Textbook Sentence: No hablo español
    Colloquial Version: Hablo un poco de español
    Meaning: I don’t speak Spanish / I speak a little Spanish
  • Textbook Sentence: ¿Puedes ayudarme?
    Colloquial Version: ¿Me echas una mano?
    Meaning: Can you help me?
  • Textbook Sentence: Estoy feliz
    Colloquial Version: Estoy contento/a
    Meaning: I’m happy
  • Textbook Sentence: Estoy enojado/a
    Colloquial Version: Estoy bravo/a
    Meaning: I’m angry
