Mastering Spanish Idioms: Speak Like a Native

Idioms add colour and humour to your speech but often differ from literal English. The Spanish idioms below will make you sound more natural. Practise them in conversation with Lexa AI—download the app on the App Store here.

IdiomHow Locals Actually Say ItHow to Pronounce ItMeaning / English Translation
