Popular Spanish Sayings and Their Meanings

  • Saying: El tiempo lo cura todo
    Meaning: Time heals everything
  • Saying: Si te caes siete veces, levántate ocho
    Meaning: If you fall seven times, get up eight
  • Saying: El que mucho abarca, poco aprieta
    Meaning: Don’t bite off more than you can chew
  • Saying: Más vale pájaro en mano, que cien volando
    Meaning: A bird in the hand is worth two in the bush
  • Saying: Dime con quién andas y te diré quién eres
    Meaning: Tell me who you’re with and I’ll tell you who you are
