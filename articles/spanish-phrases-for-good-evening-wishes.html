Spanish Phrases for Good Evening Wishes

These Spanish phrases for good evening wishes can be used interchangeably with “Buenas noches.” Practise them with Lexa AI—download the app on the App Store here.

Common Phrases

  • Buenas noches
    How locals actually say it: Buenas
    How to pronounce it: booehnahs
    Meaning: Good evening / Good night
  • Feliz noche
    How locals actually say it: Que tengas una buena noche
    How to pronounce it: kooeh tehngahs oonah booehnah nohcheh
    Meaning: Have a good night
  • Que duermas bien
    How locals actually say it: Duerme bien
    How to pronounce it: dooehrmeh beeehn
    Meaning: Sleep well
  • Gracias, igualmente
    How locals actually say it: Igualmente
    How to pronounce it: eegooahlmehnteh
    Meaning: Thanks, same to you
  • Hasta mañana
    How locals actually say it: Nos vemos mañana
    How to pronounce it: nohs behmohs mahnyahnah
    Meaning: See you tomorrow
  • Descansa
    How locals actually say it: Que descanses
    How to pronounce it: kooeh dehskahnsehs
    Meaning: Rest well
  • Que pases buena noche
    How locals actually say it: Que te vaya bien esta noche
    How to pronounce it: kooeh teh bahyah beeehn ehstah nohcheh
    Meaning: Have a good night
  • Buenas noches a todos
    How locals actually say it: Buenas a todos
    How to pronounce it: booehnahs ah tohdohs
    Meaning: Good evening, everyone
