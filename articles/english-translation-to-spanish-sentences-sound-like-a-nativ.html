English Translation to Spanish Sentences - Sound Like a Native

Here are common English sentences with their Spanish translations. Practise saying the Spanish versions with Lexa AI—download the app on the App Store here.

Common English Sentences Translated to Spanish

  • How are you?
    ¿Cómo estás?
    Pronunciation: kohmoh ehstahs
  • Good morning
    Buenos días
    Pronunciation: booehnohs deeahs
  • Good night
    Buenas noches
    Pronunciation: booehnahs nohkehs
  • Please
    Por favor
    Pronunciation: pohr fahbohr
  • Thank you
    Gracias
    Pronunciation: grahkeeahs
  • I am hungry
    Tengo hambre
    Pronunciation: tehngoh ahmbreh
  • Where is the bathroom?
    ¿Dónde está el baño?
    Pronunciation: dohndeh ehstah ehl bahnyoh
  • What time is it?
    ¿Qué hora es?
    Pronunciation: kooeh ohrah ehs
  • Do you speak English?
    ¿Hablas inglés?
    Pronunciation: ahblahs eenglehs
  • My name is John
    Me llamo John
    Pronunciation: meh llahmoh hohn
