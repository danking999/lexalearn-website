Cute Phrases in Spanish – Sound Like a Native Speaker

Use these sweet phrases to express affection and charm in your Spanish conversations. Practise them with Lexa AI—download the app on the App Store here.

Common Cute Phrases

  • Phrase: Eres mi sol
    How locals say it: Eres mi sol
    Pronunciation: ehrehs mee sohl
    Meaning: You are my sunshine
  • Phrase: Te quiero mucho
    How locals say it: Te quiero mucho
    Pronunciation: teh kooeeehroh mookoh
    Meaning: I love you very much
  • Phrase: Me haces feliz
    How locals say it: Me haces feliz
    Pronunciation: meh ahkehs fehlees
    Meaning: You make me happy
  • Phrase: Eres muy lindo/a
    How locals say it: Eres muy lindo/a
    Pronunciation: ehrehs mooee leendoh/ah
    Meaning: You are very cute
  • Phrase: Sueña conmigo
    How locals say it: Sueña conmigo
    Pronunciation: sooehnyah kohnmeegoh
    Meaning: Dream of me
  • Phrase: Eres mi media naranja
    How locals say it: Eres mi media naranja
    Pronunciation: ehrehs mee mehdeeah nahrahnhah
    Meaning: You are my other half
  • Phrase: Te extraño
    How locals say it: Te extraño
    Pronunciation: teh ehkstrahnyoh
    Meaning: I miss you
  • Phrase: Eres mi tesoro
    How locals say it: Eres mi tesoro
    Pronunciation: ehrehs mee tehsohroh
    Meaning: You are my treasure
  • Phrase: Me encantas
    How locals say it: Me encantas
    Pronunciation: meh ehnkahntahs
    Meaning: I like you a lot
  • Phrase: Eres mi cielo
    How locals say it: Eres mi cielo
    Pronunciation: ehrehs mee keeehloh
    Meaning: You are my heaven
