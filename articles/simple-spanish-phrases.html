Essential Simple Spanish Phrases for Easy Communication

People looking for simple Spanish phrases generally want easy, short expressions. Memorising simple sentences can significantly ease communication while traveling or socializing. Here are some essential phrases to get you started:

Key Simple Phrases

  • Estoy bien, gracias (I’m fine, thanks)
  • Hasta mañana (See you tomorrow)
  • No lo sé (I don’t know)

Emotional Expressions

A beginner-focused resource adds emotional expressions, which can help convey feelings accurately:

  • Me gusta (I like it)
  • Estoy triste (I’m sad)
