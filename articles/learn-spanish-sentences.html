Learn Spanish Sentences: Build Conversational Fluency

To learn Spanish sentences, it's essential to build conversational fluency by focusing on whole sentences rather than isolated words. These sample sentences and their variants can be used in different situations. Practise them with Lexa AI—download the app on the App Store here.

Useful Sentence Structures and Variations

  • Sentence (textbook): Tengo … años
    How locals actually say it: Tengo …
    Meaning / English translation: I am … years old
  • Sentence (textbook): Estoy enojado/a
    How locals actually say it: Estoy bravo/a
    Meaning / English translation: I’m angry
  • Sentence (textbook): ¿Puedo pagar con tarjeta?
    How locals actually say it: ¿Aceptan tarjeta?
    Meaning / English translation: Can I pay with card?
  • Sentence (textbook): Soy de …
    How locals actually say it: Soy de…
    Meaning / English translation: I am from …
  • Sentence (textbook): Estoy alérgico/a a …
    How locals actually say it: Soy alérgico/a a …
    Meaning / English translation: I’m allergic to …
  • Sentence (textbook): ¿Hablas inglés?
    How locals actually say it: ¿Habla inglés?
    Meaning / English translation: Do you speak English?
  • Sentence (textbook): Estoy perdido/a
    How locals actually say it: Estoy desorientado/a
    Meaning / English translation: I’m lost / I’m disoriented
