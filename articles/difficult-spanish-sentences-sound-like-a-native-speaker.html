Practice with Difficult Spanish Sentences

Here are some phrases that will help you sound like a native speaker:

  • Aunque la mona se vista de seda, mona se queda.
    Pronunciation: ahoonkooeh lah mohnah seh beestah deh sehdah mohnah seh kooehdah
    Meaning: Even if a monkey dresses in silk, it's still a monkey.
  • Si hubiera sabido que venías, habría preparado algo.
    Pronunciation: see oobeeehrah sahbeedoh kooeh behneeahs ahbreeah prehpahrahdoh ahlgoh
    Meaning: If I had known you were coming, I would have prepared something.
  • Cuando tengas tiempo, dime qué piensas de la película que vimos la semana pasada.
    Pronunciation: kooahndoh tehngahs teeehmpoh deemeh kooeh peeehnsahs deh lah pehleekoolah kooeh beemohs lah sehmahnah pahsahdah
    Meaning: When you have time, tell me what you think of the movie we saw last week.
  • No solo voy a viajar a España, sino que también quiero aprender catalán.
    Pronunciation: noh sohloh bohee ah beeahhahr ah ehspahnyah seenoh kooeh tahmbeeehn kooeeehroh ahprehndehr kahtahlahn
    Meaning: Not only am I going to travel to Spain, I also want to learn Catalan.
  • El que mucho abarca, poco aprieta.
    Pronunciation: ehl kooeh mookoh ahbahrkah pohkoh ahpreeehtah
    Meaning: If you try to do too much, you end up achieving little.

More Examples of Difficult Spanish Sentences

  • Quisiera saber si puedes ayudarme a organizar el evento la próxima semana.
    Pronunciation: kooeeseeehrah sahbehr see pooehdehs aheeoodahrmeh ah ohrgahneesahr ehl ehbehntoh lah prohkseemah sehmahnah
    Meaning: I would like to know if you can help me organize the event next week.
  • Nunca había escuchado una historia tan impresionante como la que me contaste ayer.
    Pronunciation: noonkah ahbeeah ehskookahdoh oonah eestohreeah tahn eemprehseeohnahnteh kohmoh lah kooeh meh kohntahsteh aheeehr
    Meaning: I had never heard such an impressive story as the one you told me yesterday.
  • Si no fuera por ti, no habría terminado la tarea a tiempo.
    Pronunciation: see noh fooehrah pohr tee noh ahbreeah tehrmeenahdoh lah tahrehah ah teeehmpoh
    Meaning: If it weren’t for you, I wouldn’t have finished the homework on time.
  • La tecnología avanza tan rápido que a veces cuesta mantenerse al día.
    Pronunciation: lah tehknohlohgeeah ahbahnsah tahn rahpeedoh kooeh ah behkehs kooehstah mahntehnehrseh ahl deeah
    Meaning: Technology advances so quickly that sometimes it’s hard to keep up.
  • No importa lo difícil que sea, siempre hay una solución si buscas con paciencia.
    Pronunciation: noh eempohrtah loh deefeekeel kooeh sehah seeehmpreh ahee oonah sohlookeeohn see booskahs kohn pahkeeehnkeeah
    Meaning: No matter how hard it is, there is always a solution if you search patiently.
